  • Situation: High inquiry volume caused delayed responses and missed follow-ups because tracking was inconsistent.
  • Actions: Standardized Zendesk logging, categorized by urgency/next action, used a simple tracker for pending cases, followed up proactively and closed the loop.

  • Result: Better visibility of open issues; fewer missed follow-ups; faster and more consistent responses.

  • Result: Client completed the process independently and felt confident using the system again.
